FAQ


Global Catalogue - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)


The customer service phone number that appears on the Contact Us page is for award-related inquiries only. For any programme-specific questions, please contact your programme administrator.   

  1. What should I do if I receive a defective/damaged item from you?
    A1. In the unlikely event that you receive a damaged/defective item, please Contact Us as soon as possible and within 3 business days of receiving the item to obtain an authorisation for return. After we authorise a return, we will provide additional instructions for replacement.


  2. Is there any way to place an order other than online?
    A2. No. Currently, online ordering is the only option we offer.


  3. How safe is my personal information with you?
    A3. Online ordering via our web site is encrypted using a SSL (Secure Sockets Layer) protocol. The site is a VeriSign secure site. For more information, please read our Privacy Statement.. 


  4. Am I responsible for working out the taxes, duties, customs and/or shipping costs for my order?
    A4. No. The amount of points required for an item includes these costs.


  5. How will my order be delivered?
    A5. Your order will be delivered via commercial courier services.


  6. I have placed an order. How do I know that you have received it?
    A6. When you place an order, you will receive a return e-mail to your registered e-mail address from the Rewards service provider indicating order details along with an order transaction number. This information is an acknowledgement from us that we have received and are processing your order.


  7. Are my awards under a warranty?
    A7. Most products are backed by a manufacturer warranty. You are encouraged to contact the manufacturer directly regarding all warranty related inquiries.


  8. Can I cancel an order I have placed?
    A8. No, we cannot cancel an order that you have placed online. Please be sure to verify your order details and delivery preferences before placing an order. We are not responsible for any delays or additional charges incurred as a result of incorrect shipping information.


  9. What is your return policy?
    A9. Only damaged or defective merchandise may be returned.


  10. How do I contact Customer Service?
    A10. Please refer to the Contact Us section of this website.


  11. Why do you need my e-mail address?
    A11. The information we collect is required to confirm your order and provide shipping/delivery status on applicable items. For additional information, please read our Privacy Statement


  12. How long will it take to receive my order?
    A12. Please allow 4-6 weeks for processing and delivery of your order.


  13. How do I change my address information?
    A13.
    • To change the address information appearing as your ‘registered address’, please contact your programme administrator.


    • To ship to an alternate address within the same country, please select the alternate delivery address option during redemption.


    • Once an order has been placed, we regret that delivery information cannot be changed.

  14. How often do you update products and pricing?
    A14. To keep our selections fresh and innovative, we are continually looking for items to enhance our catalogue selections. This includes adding new items, new models, removing discontinued items and pricing reviews. It is difficult to predict the timing of these changes, but rest assured that these are areas that are reviewed on an ongoing basis.


  15. Can I exchange or transfer my points?
    A15. Rewards are not transferable and have no cash value.


  16. Will I be taxed on the value of my award(s)?
    A16. Liability for taxes on any award earnings pursuant to the programme (such as any taxes on fringe benefits) shall be the sole responsibility of the participant.


  17. Will I receive the exact reward item as shown on the site?
    A17. Item images and descriptions are for reference, but we will make every effort to provide the exact item as presented. In the event that an item becomes unavailable, we reserve the right to provide an item of equal or greater value and quality.
